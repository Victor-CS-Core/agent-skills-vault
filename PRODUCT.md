# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Developers, makers, and tinkerers managing their personal agent skills. Their context is focused tool-curation, needing quick search, import, and copying of skill commands.

## Product Purpose
A local-first personal library for storing, browsing, and searching favorite AI agent skills from GitHub and skills.sh. Success means instant access and minimal friction when finding and executing a skill.

## Brand Personality
Developer-focused, cyberpunk/neon-inspired, modern, sleek. Deep dark mode with vibrant neon accents (purple, cyan, pink), glassmorphism, and atmospheric glow.

## Anti-references
Light mode, corporate SaaS cream, heavily rounded playful UI, bland utilitarian admin panels.

## Design Principles
- **Speed Over Everything:** Search and copy must feel instant.
- **Atmospheric Focus:** Use glow and color to guide attention, not overwhelm it.
- **Developer First:** Information density, monospace typography for technical details, keyboard shortcuts.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Standard web accessibility (keyboard nav, decent contrast) with a focus on high legibility of technical content.
